What’s Special About This Drink?

The Ginger Turmeric Shot combines two of nature’s most powerful roots — ginger and turmeric — in a concentrated, easy-to-consume format.

Lemon adds freshness, honey brings natural smoothness, and a touch of pepper helps activate the blend. Shake & Gulp Ginger Turmeric Shot delivers this age-old combination in a modern, convenient form. Since natural ingredients settle, every bottle is meant to be shaken well before drinking.
